Crop: Rice

Season:
- Kharif crop
- Sowing time: June to July
- Harvest time: October to November

Climate:
- Temperature: 20°C to 35°C
- Requires high humidity and rainfall

Water:
- Needs standing water in field
- Maintain water level during growth

Soil:
- Clayey soil is ideal
- Fields should retain water

Seed:
- Use high-yield varieties
- Examples: Swarna, IR64

Fertilizer:
- Use NPK fertilizers
- Apply nitrogen in multiple stages

Common Pests:
- Stem borer
- Brown planthopper

Diseases:
- Blast disease
- Bacterial leaf blight

Advice:
- Maintain proper drainage
- Avoid excessive fertilizer use
- Use resistant varieties

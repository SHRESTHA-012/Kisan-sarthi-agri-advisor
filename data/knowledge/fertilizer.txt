Topic: Fertilizers

Types:
- Organic fertilizers: compost, manure
- Chemical fertilizers: urea, DAP, potash

Nutrients:
- Nitrogen (N): promotes leaf growth
- Phosphorus (P): helps root development
- Potassium (K): improves overall plant health

Application:
- Apply based on soil test
- Use split application for better results

Organic Methods:
- Use compost and green manure
- Improves soil structure

Common Fertilizers:
- Urea
- DAP (Diammonium Phosphate)
- MOP (Muriate of Potash)

Advice:
- Avoid excessive fertilizer use
- Apply at correct time
- Mix properly in soil

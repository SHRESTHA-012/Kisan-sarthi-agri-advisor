Topic: Pest Control

Common Pests:
- Aphids
- Whiteflies
- Caterpillars
- Stem borers

Signs of Pest Attack:
- Yellowing leaves
- Holes in leaves
- Sticky substance on plants

Organic Control:
- Neem oil spray
- Garlic and chili spray
- Use natural predators

Chemical Control:
- Use pesticides only if necessary
- Follow recommended dosage
- Do not overuse chemicals

Preventive Measures:
- Regular field inspection
- Crop rotation
- Clean field after harvest

Advice:
- Spray in early morning or evening
- Wear protection while spraying
- Avoid spraying during rain

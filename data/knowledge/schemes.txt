Topic: Government Schemes for Farmers (India/Bihar)

PM-KISAN:
- Provides ₹6000 per year to farmers
- Paid in three installments

Soil Health Card:
- Provides information about soil nutrients
- Helps in proper fertilizer use

Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana:
- Crop insurance scheme
- Protects against crop loss

Kisan Credit Card:
- Provides easy loans for farming

Subsidies:
- Available for seeds, fertilizers, and equipment

Advice:
- Register through local agriculture office
- Keep documents ready

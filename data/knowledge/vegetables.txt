Topic: Vegetable Farming

Common Vegetables:
- Tomato
- Potato
- Brinjal
- Cauliflower
- Okra

Climate:
- Most vegetables need sunlight and moderate temperature
- Avoid extreme heat and frost

Soil:
- Fertile loamy soil with good drainage is ideal

Irrigation:
- Water regularly
- Avoid overwatering

Fertilizer:
- Use compost and balanced fertilizers
- Organic manure improves soil quality

Common Pests:
- Aphids
- Fruit borers
- Whiteflies

Diseases:
- Wilt disease
- Leaf spot
- Powdery mildew

Advice:
- Use healthy seeds
- Remove infected plants quickly
- Practice crop rotation
- Use mulching to conserve moisture

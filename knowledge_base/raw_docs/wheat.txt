# Topic: Wheat (Gehun) — Complete Advisory for Bihar Farmers
# Language: Hinglish
# Region: Bihar, India

---

## Gehun ki Kheti — Bihar ke liye Puri Jankari

Gehun Bihar ki sabse important Rabi fasal hai. November-December mein boya jata hai aur March-April mein katai hoti hai. Patna, Gaya, Rohtas, Bhojpur, Buxar, Saran aur Muzaffarpur jile mein sabse zyada gehun ugaya jata hai.

---

## Behtareen Kisme (Best Varieties for Bihar)

HD-2967: Bihar ke liye sabse recommended variety. 120 din mein taiyar, 20 quintal per acre yield. Rust rog se ladne ki kshamta achhi hai.

PBW-550: Jaldi pakne wali variety (115 din). Jo kisan der se bote hain unke liye behtar hai.

HI-8498 (Malav Shakti): 22 quintal per acre yield deti hai. Protein zyada hota hai aur sukhe mein bhi theek dhang se ug jati hai.

Der se bowane ke liye (December ke baad): K-307 aur NW-1014 kisme use karein. Ye temperature badne par bhi kharab nahi hoti.

---

## Bowane ka Sahi Samay aur Tarika

Sahi samay: 15 November se 15 December tak. Jaldi bowane se rust aur aphid ka khatra badh jata hai. Der se bowane se terminal heat stress hoti hai.

Beej ki matra: 40 kg per acre. Line ki doori 22.5 cm rakho. Gehun ki geharai 5 cm se zyada mat karo.

Bowane se pehle beej ko fungicide se upcharit karein: Carboxin 75WP (Vitavax) 2.5g per kg beej — isse Loose Smut rog nahi lagta.

---

## Khad Prabandhan

Bowane ke samay: 50 kg DAP + 20 kg MOP per acre zameen mein milaein.

Pehle paani ke baad (20-25 din mein): 30 kg Urea per acre dein.

Doosre paani ke baad (40-45 din mein): 20 kg Urea per acre dein.

Bihar ki mitti mein Zinc ki kami aam hai. Bowane se pehle 5 kg Zinc Sulphate per acre zaroor milaein. Isse dono dhaan aur gehun mein faida hota hai.

---

## Sinchai Prabandhan

Gehun mein 6 paani dene chahiye:

1. Crown Root Initiation (20-25 din baad) — yeh sabse zaroori paani hai
2. Tillering (40-45 din baad)
3. Jointing (60-65 din baad)
4. Flowering (80-85 din baad)
5. Grain Filling (100-105 din baad)
6. Dough Stage (115-120 din baad)

Agar paani ki kami ho to pehle 3 paani zaroor dein. Terminal drought se zyada nuksan hota hai.

---

## Kharpatwar Prabandhan (Weed Management)

Gehun mein Bathua (Chenopodium), Jangli Jai (Wild Oat) aur Hirankuri common kharpatwar hain.

Bowane ke 25-30 din baad Isoproturon 75WP (400g/acre) ya Clodinafop 15WP (160g/acre) spray karein. Spray ke samay mitti mein naami honi chahiye.

---

## Pramukh Rog aur Keede

Rataun (Rust): Bihar mein Yellow Rust aur Brown Rust dono paaye jate hain. January-March mein thandi aur barish ke baad aata hai. Patte par peele ya laal powdery dhabbey dikhtey hain. Propiconazole 25EC (200ml/acre) spray karein. Resist varieties use karo to yeh problem hi nahi aayegi.

Khula Kanda (Loose Smut): Pure baal ki jagah kala powdery daag dikhta hai. Yeh beej ke andar chhupta hai. Iska ilaaj sirf beej upchar hai — bowane se pehle Vitavax se upcharit beej hi lagao.

Mahu (Aphid): Chote hare-kaale keede patte aur shooths par dikhte hain. December-January mein fog ke baad zyada hote hain. 10 aphid per shoot milne par Imidacloprid 17.8SL (75ml/acre) spray karein.

---

## Katai

April mein jab dane sakht ho jaayein aur naami 20% se kam ho tab combine se katai karein. Katai mein der mat karein nahi to dane jhad jaate hain aur chamak bhi kam hoti hai.

Katai ke baad dane ko khule mein sukhaein. Bhandaran ke liye 12% se kam naami zaroori hai.

---

## MSP aur Bikri

Gehun ka MSP 2024-25 mein Rs. 2275 per quintal hai. Bihar mein FCI aur BSFCSC April 1 se June 30 tak kharidi karte hain. Apna registration dbtagriculture.bihar.gov.in par karein.

Crop: Wheat

Season:
- Rabi crop
- Sowing time: October to December
- Harvest time: March to April

Climate:
- Ideal temperature: 10°C to 25°C
- Requires cool growing season and warm harvesting

Soil:
- Well-drained loamy soil is best
- Avoid waterlogged fields

Seed:
- Use certified seeds
- Common varieties: HD-2967, PBW-343

Fertilizer:
- Nitrogen (N), Phosphorus (P), Potassium (K)
- Apply urea in split doses

Irrigation:
- First irrigation after 20-25 days
- Total 4-5 irrigations required

Common Pests:
- Aphids
- Termites

Diseases:
- Rust
- Smut

Advice:
- Avoid over-irrigation
- Use proper spacing between plants
- Monitor pest attacks regularly

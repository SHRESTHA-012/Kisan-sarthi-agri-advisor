## Bihar ki Khaas Faslen — GI Tag aur Vishesh Pahchaan

Bihar mein kuch aisi faslen hain jo poori duniya mein mash-hoor hain aur jinhe Geographical Indication (GI) tag mila hai. In faslon ki kheti karna zyada munafa deta hai aur kisan ko brand ki taaqat milti hai. In mein Makhana, Shahi Litchi, Zardalu Aam aur Katarni Chawal sabse aham hain.

---

## Makhana (Fox Nut / Lotus Seeds) — Bihar ka Sunahara Khazana

Makhana Bihar ki sabse unique fasal hai. Darbhanga, Madhubani, Sitamarhi, Supaul aur Saharsa jile mein 80% se zyada Makhana ugaya jaata hai. Poori duniya ka 90% Makhana Bihar se aata hai. Ise "GI Tag" mila hai — Mithila Makhana ke naam se.

Makhana kya hota hai: Yeh kamal (Euryale ferox) ke beej hote hain jo taalabon aur jheeloun mein ugaye jaate hain.

Kaise ugaya jaata hai:
February-March mein beej taalabon mein daale jaate hain.
Paudhon ko 2-5 feet gehra paani chahiye.
July-October mein beej talab ki tali mein jama hote hain.
Kisan talab mein ghuskar haath se beej nikaalte hain — bahut mehnati kaam.
Beej ko baaloon par bhuna jaata hai — phir hathode se toda jata hai — yahi Makhana banta hai.

Paidawar: 10-12 quintal raw gatta per acre. Processing ke baad 3-4 quintal phoola Makhana.

Bhav: Makhana ka baazaar mein bhav Rs. 400-1000 per kg tak hota hai (quality ke hisaab se). Organic aur export quality ka bhav zyada.

Sarkaari Sahayata:
National Research Centre for Makhana (NRCM) Darbhanga mein hai — technical support aur training milti hai.
NABARD aur Bihar sarkar Makhana processing unit par subsidy deti hai.

Challenges: Talab ki zameen chahiye, processing bahut mehnat wali hai. SHG (Self Help Groups) milkar processing karen to zyada faida.

## Shahi Litchi — Muzaffarpur ki Shan

Muzaffarpur ki Shahi Litchi duniya mein mashoor hai aur ise GI tag mila hai. Vaishali, Sheohar, Sitamarhi aur East Champaran mein bhi achhi litchi hoti hai.

Shahi Litchi kya hai: Ek khaas variety jo sirf Muzaffarpur ke aaspas ugti hai. Patlaa chilka, gehre laal rang, bahut meetha, khushboodar. China variety se bilkul alag.

Ped lagana:
Gooty (air layering) se tayaar podhe lagaein — July-August best time.
Doori: 8x8 meter ya 10x10 meter.
Ek acre mein 50 ped lagte hain.
5-6 saal mein phal aana shuru.

Kaise dekhbhaal karein:
Har saal FYM 50kg + Urea 1kg + SSP 1.5kg + MOP 750g per ped.
Sinchai: Flowering se pehle December-January mein thordi sinchai se phool achhe lagte hain. Fruit setting ke baad regular sinchai zaroori.
Pruning: Phal todne ke turant baad dead aur crossing branches kaat do — agale saal ka phool usi cutting par aayega.

Sabse Badi Samasya — Fruit Cracking:
Phal pakne ke samay agar achanak barish aaye ya irregular sinchai ho to Litchi ka chilka fat jaata hai. Iske liye: Drip irrigation lagaein. Calcium Nitrate spray (0.5%) phal badhne ki stage mein.

Harvesting: May-June mein phal aata hai. Ped se toda aur 2-3 din ke andar becho — shelf life sirf 3 din hoti hai.

Export: Shahi Litchi Europe aur Middle East export hoti hai. APEDA (Agricultural and Processed Food Products Export Development Authority) se sampark karo.

## Zardalu Aam (Mango) — Bhagalpur ka GI Gift

Bhagalpur ka Zardalu Aam aur Malda ka Fazli Aam Bihar mein mashoor hain. Zardalu ko GI tag mila hai.

Zardalu: Chhota aam, sone jaisa peela, bahut meetha, fiber nahi. Sirf 3-4 hafte milta hai (June mein). Isliye bahut keemat hoti hai — Rs. 100-300 per kg tak.

Aam ki Dekhbhaal:
Saal mein ek baar February mein pruning.
Flowering ke samay (January-February) mowr spraying mat karo. Powdery mildew ke liye Sulfur 80WP spray.
Mango Hopper: Flowering ke samay Imidacloprid 0.5ml per litre paani ka spray karo.
Fruit Fly: Phal pakne par Methyl Eugenol traps lagaein.

## Katarni Chawal — Bhagalpur ka Sugandhit Chawal

Katarni ek local desi rice variety hai jo sirf Bhagalpur aur Banka jile mein ugti hai. GI tag mila hai.

Khaasiyat: Bahut sugandhit (aromatic), paka hone par khil jaata hai. Puja aur special occasions ke liye use hota hai.

Kheti: Kharif mein ugaaya jaata hai. 155-160 din mein taiyaar. Yield kam — 12-15 quintal per acre — lekin bhav bahut zyada milta hai.

Bhav: Normal dhaan Rs. 2300 per quintal ka MSP, lekin Katarni Rs. 5000-8000 per quintal tak bikata hai.

Challenge: Yeh variety blast rog ke liye sensitive hai. Tricyclazole ka preventive spray zaroori hai.

## Madhubani Maithil Cuisine Crops — Cultural Importance

Makhana, Katarni aur Zardalu teen GI crops hain jo Maithil aur Bhojpuri sanskriti se gehra rishta rakhte hain. In faslon ki cultivation sirf kheti nahi — ek cultural heritage ki raksha hai.

Kisan Seedha Bechein — FPO Banayein:
Makhana aur Litchi ke liye FPO (Farmer Producer Organization) banana bahut faydemand hai.
Milkar processing, packaging aur branding karo.
NABARD FPO formation ke liye Rs. 15 lakh deta hai — 3 saal mein.
Amazon, Flipkart ya local organic stores par seedha becho — 30-40% zyada bhav milta hai.

## Makhaana / Litchi — ATMA Training

Bihar Agriculture Department aur ATMA (Agricultural Technology Management Agency) Makhana aur Litchi par training programs chalate hain. Apne jile ke ATMA ya KVK (Krishi Vigyan Kendra) mein sampark karein.

Har jile mein ek KVK hai — wahan free demonstrations, quality seeds aur technical guidance milti hai.

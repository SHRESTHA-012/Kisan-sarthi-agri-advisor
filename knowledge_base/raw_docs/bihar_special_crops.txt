Topic: Major Crops in Bihar

Main Crops:
- Rice
- Wheat
- Maize
- Pulses
- Sugarcane

Rice in Bihar:
- Major kharif crop
- Grown widely during monsoon season

Maize in Bihar:
- Bihar is one of the top maize-producing states in India
- Used for food and poultry feed

Litchi:
- Bihar is famous for Muzaffarpur litchi
- Requires warm and humid climate

Makhana:
- Grown mainly in north Bihar ponds
- Bihar is a leading producer of makhana

Sugarcane:
- Important cash crop
- Requires regular irrigation

Pulses:
- Gram, lentil, and arhar are common
- Improve soil fertility through nitrogen fixation

Challenges:
- Floods in north Bihar
- Drought in some regions
- Pest attacks during humid weather

Advice:
- Follow weather forecasts
- Use flood-resistant and drought-resistant varieties
- Adopt modern farming techniques

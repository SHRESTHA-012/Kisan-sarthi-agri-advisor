Topic: Weather-based Farming Advice

Rain:
- Ensure proper drainage in heavy rain
- Avoid waterlogging

Drought:
- Use drought-resistant crops
- Practice water conservation

Cold:
- Protect crops from frost
- Use light irrigation to reduce damage

Heat:
- Irrigate crops regularly
- Use mulching to retain moisture

Wind:
- Protect crops using barriers
- Avoid spraying during strong winds

Advice:
- Check weather forecast regularly
- Plan sowing and irrigation accordingly

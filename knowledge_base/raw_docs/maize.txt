Crop: Maize

Season:
- Grown in Kharif, Rabi, and Zaid seasons
- Main sowing time: June to July

Climate:
- Temperature: 18°C to 27°C
- Requires moderate rainfall and sunlight

Soil:
- Well-drained fertile loamy soil is best
- Avoid waterlogged areas

Seed:
- Use certified hybrid seeds
- Common varieties: DHM-117, HQPM-1

Fertilizer:
- Requires Nitrogen, Phosphorus, and Potassium
- Apply nitrogen in split doses

Irrigation:
- Irrigate during flowering and grain filling stages
- Avoid excess water

Common Pests:
- Fall armyworm
- Stem borer

Diseases:
- Leaf blight
- Downy mildew

Advice:
- Monitor for pest attacks regularly
- Maintain proper spacing between plants
- Use crop rotation to reduce disease

## Khad Prabandhan — Sahi Jankari

Khet mein sahi matra mein, sahi samay par, sahi tarike se khad dena — yahi achhi paidawar ka raaz hai. Zyada khad dene se paisa barbad hota hai, zameen kharab hoti hai aur fasal mein rog-keede zyada lagte hain. Kam khad se paidawar ghatti hai.

Mitti jaanch (Soil Testing) karwaein pehle — phir uske hisaab se khad dein. Yeh sabse scientific aur sasta tarika hai.

## Teen Mukhya Poshak Tatva (NPK)

Nitrogen (N) — Neel rang ka poshak:
Kaam: Patte aur tanon ki badhwaar ke liye. Patte hare rehte hain.
Kami ke lakshan: Neeche ke purane patte pehle peele padte hain, upar se neeche ki taraf aata hai.
Source: Urea (46% N), DAP (18% N), Ammonium Sulphate (21% N)
Dhyan: Zyada N se rog-keede badhte hain. Kharif mein dhaan-makka mein zyada zaroorat. Rabi mein gehun mein 3 baar split mein dein.

Phosphorus (P) — Jadon ka poshak:
Kaam: Jadein mazboot banata hai, jaldi pakwaaw, phool-phal badhata hai.
Kami ke lakshan: Patte neele-laal rang ke ho jaate hain. Paudha chota rehta hai. Jadein kamzor.
Source: DAP (46% P2O5), SSP (16% P2O5), NP fertilizers
Dhyan: Zameen mein mix karein — surface par chhodne se faida nahi. Ek baar bowane par dena kaafi hota hai.

Potassium (K) — Mazbooti ka poshak:
Kaam: Rog se ladne ki kshamta, sukhe mein sahayata, dana bhari hota hai, quality achhi hoti hai.
Kami ke lakshan: Patte ke kinare se bhure-jale hue lagte hain. Daane halke hote hain. Paudha kamzor.
Source: MOP/KCl (60% K2O), SOP (50% K2O — sulphur bhi deta hai)
Dhyan: Bihar mein potassium ki kami badhti ja rahi hai. Dhan aur sugarcane mein zaroori.

## Mukhya Upyog — Crop-wise Fertilizer Schedule

Dhaan (Paddy):
Bowane par: 25 kg DAP + 15 kg MOP + 5 kg Zinc Sulphate
21 din baad: 25 kg Urea
45 din baad: 20 kg Urea
Total: 45 kg Urea + 25 kg DAP + 15 kg MOP per acre

Gehun (Wheat):
Bowane par: 50 kg DAP + 20 kg MOP
Pehle paani par: 30 kg Urea
Doosre paani par: 20 kg Urea
Total: 50 kg Urea + 50 kg DAP + 20 kg MOP per acre

Makka (Maize):
Bowane par: 40 kg DAP + 20 kg MOP
V4 stage: 30 kg Urea
V8 stage: 30 kg Urea
Total: 60 kg Urea + 40 kg DAP + 20 kg MOP per acre

Sarson (Mustard):
Bowane par: 25 kg DAP + 15 kg MOP + 8 kg Sulphur
30 din baad: 25 kg Urea
Total: 25 kg Urea + 25 kg DAP + 15 kg MOP per acre

Aalu (Potato):
Bowane par: 60 kg DAP + 60 kg MOP
Earthing up par: 40 kg Urea

## Sukshma Poshak Tatva (Micronutrients) — Bihar ki Khas Zaroorat

Bihar ki zameen mein micronutrient ki kami bahut common hai. Iski wajah se fasal ki badi quality aur quantity dono prabhavit hoti hai.

Zinc (Jasta) ki Kami — Sabse Common:
Bihar ke 60-70% khetons mein Zinc ki kami hai.
Lakshan: Naye patte ke beech peeli-safed dhariyan (Khaira rog in paddy). Patta chota rehta hai. Paudha stunted.
Ilaaj: Zinc Sulphate 21% (5 kg per acre) zameen mein bowane se pehle dein.
Turant ilaaj (foliar): Zinc Sulphate 0.5% (5g per litre paani) spray karein.
Dhyan: Har 2-3 saal mein Zinc dena chahiye.

Boron (Boran) ki Kami:
Phool gobi, sarson, sunflower, sugarcane mein zyada hoti hai.
Lakshan: Gobi mein andar se kaala ya brown (Browning). Paudhon mein phool achhi tarah nahi lagte. Sarson mein pods nahi bantey.
Ilaaj: Borax (11% B) 1 kg per acre bowane se pehle. Foliar: Boric acid 0.2% spray.

Iron (Loha) ki Kami:
Aloo, chawal mein. Alkaline ya waterlogged mitti mein zyada.
Lakshan: Naye patte peele ho jaate hain lekin naasein (veins) hare rehti hain (interveinal chlorosis).
Ilaaj: Ferrous Sulphate 0.5% spray (5g per litre) 2-3 baar karein. Zameen mein acidification karein (sulphur dale).

Sulphur (Gandhak) ki Kami:
Sarson, pyaaz, lahsun, dalo mein bahut zaroori.
Lakshan: Naye patte peele. Sarson mein phool achhe nahi lagte.
Ilaaj: SSP (Single Super Phosphate) use karein — isme P ke saath S bhi milta hai. Ya Ammonium Sulphate (21% S) use karein.

## Jaivik Khad (Organic Fertilizers) — Mitti ka Poshan

Gobar Ki Khad (FYM — Farm Yard Manure):
Sabse achha jaivik khad. 4-5 ton per acre saalik dene se mitti ki sehat bahut achhi rehti hai.
NPK: 0.5% N, 0.25% P, 0.5% K — chemical se kam lekin long-term benefit zyada.
Kaise: 2-3 mahine purana gala-sada gobar dein. Kaccha gobar rog-keede laata hai.

Vermicompost (Kechua Khad):
FYM se 3-4 guna zyada powerful. 2 ton per acre kaafi hai.
Fayde: Mitti mein hawa-paani dharna badhata hai, useful microbes badhate hain.
Bihar mein KVK aur ATMA se subsidized rate par milta hai.

Jeevamrit:
Desi cow ke gobar-mutra se ghar par banaya jaata hai. Jeevit microbes deta hai zameen ko.
Kaise banayein: 10kg desi gaay ka gobar + 10L mutra + 1kg chana aata + 1kg gud + 1 mutthi mitti. 200L paani mein mila kar 7 din rakhein, roz hilaayein. Phir 200L per acre sinchai ke paani mein mila kar dein.
Cost: Lagbhag free. Bahut effective.

Neem Khali (Neem Cake):
100-150 kg per acre zameen mein milao. Nematodes aur soil pests ko maarta hai. Nitrogen bhi deta hai.

Green Manure (Hari Khad):
Dhaincha ya Sunn Hemp ugaao aur 45-50 din mein jot do. Ek crop mein 80-90 kg N per hectare milta hai — almost ek bori urea ke barabar.

## Biofertilizers — Jeevanu Khad

Rhizobium: Hari dal, arhar, masur, chana ke beej ko bowane se pehle lagao. Rhizobium N2 gas ko seedha nitrogen mein badalta hai. Isse 25-30 kg N per hectare ki bachat hoti hai.
Kaise: 200g Rhizobium packet + thoda paani + chini mila kar paste banao. Beej par lagao aur chhaya mein sukhao. Usi roz boo do.

PSB (Phosphate Solubilizing Bacteria): Zameen mein stuck phosphorus ko ghol deta hai aur paudhon ko uplabdh karata hai. 200g packet per acre, beej upchar ya zameen mein milao.

Azotobacter: Dhaan, gehun, makka ke liye. N fix karta hai. 200g per acre.

Note: Biofertilizers ko sun se bachao. Chemical fertilizer ke saath mix mat karo. Expiry date check karo.

## Mitti Jaanch (Soil Testing) — Kaise Karwaein

Bihar mein free ya nominal rate par mitti jaanch hoti hai.

Kahaan karwaein:
- Block Agriculture Office (muft)
- Krishi Vigyan Kendra (KVK)
- Bihar Soil Testing Lab, Patna
- ICAR-RCER, Patna

Mitti kaise lein:
Khet mein 8-10 jagahon se V-shape se 0-15cm gehra sample lein. Sab milao. 500g sample ek polythene mein bandh karein. Label lagao — naam, khet ki jagah, fasal ka naam.

Report mein kya milega: pH, EC, OC (organic carbon), N, P, K, Zinc, Boron.

Soil Health Card scheme ke tahat har kisan ko muft jaanch milti hai. Apne block office mein avedan karein.

## Urea ka Sahi Upyog — Paisa Bachao

Urea sabse sasta N source hai (46% N) lekin bahut zyada waste hota hai agar galat tarike se dein.

Do's:
- Bhaari paani ya barish ke turant baad dein (zameen namdar ho)
- Undi ya kharpatwar ke paas mat dein
- Shaam ko dein — subah ki dhoop mein ammonia ud jaata hai
- Split mein dein — ek baar mein sab mat dein

Don'ts:
- Khade paani mein mat daalein (NH3 loss hoga)
- Teez dhoop mein mat daalein
- Zyada mat dein — sirf ETL ke hisaab se

Neem Coated Urea: Sarkari neem coated urea hi kharidein. Isme N dheere-dheere nikalta hai, 10-15% zyada N-use efficiency milti hai.
